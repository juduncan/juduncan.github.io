Land of Lings is a strategy game similar to red remover. The bad Lings have invaded the land and refuse to leave. The good news is there are good lings too. The good lings must push the bad lings off the map and restore order to the land. Using only their environment the good lings have an uphill battle. Luckily the good lings have and intersting environment. Blow up bombs, spook ghosts, and knock those bad lings back to where they belong in Land of the Lings.
